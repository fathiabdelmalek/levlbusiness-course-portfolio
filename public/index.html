Animals I Treat

  • Dogs

    From puppies to seniors, I provide comprehensive care including wellness exams, vaccinations, dental care, and treatment for various conditions. Special care is given to each breed's unique needs.

  • Cats

    Expert feline care and treatment including wellness exams, vaccinations, dental care, and management of feline-specific conditions. I understand cats' unique behaviors and how to make vet visits less stressful.

  • Birds

    Gentle handling and health checkups for avian friends. I provide specialized care for various bird species, including nutritional guidance, beak and nail trimming, and treatment of common avian conditions.

  • Rabbits

    Specialized care for rabbits and small mammals. Services include dental checks, nutritional counseling, spay/neuter procedures, and treatment of common conditions affecting these delicate creatures.

  • Hamsters

    Tiny friends deserve big care too. I offer specialized services for hamsters and other pocket pets including health assessments, nutritional guidance, and treatment for common conditions.

In-House Medications

  • PetCalm

    A natural supplement designed to relieve anxiety and stress in dogs and cats. Especially useful for thunderstorms, fireworks, travel, or separation anxiety. Contains a blend of calming herbs and vitamins.

  • FurPlus

    Comprehensive multivitamins for healthy fur and skin. This supplement provides essential nutrients to promote a shiny coat, reduce shedding, and support skin health for dogs and cats of all ages.

  • WormAway

    Safe and effective anti-parasitic medication for small mammals. Formulated specifically for the sensitive digestive systems of rabbits, guinea pigs, and other small pets to eliminate common internal parasites.

  • FeatherBoost

    Specialized supplements for bird health that support immune function, feather quality, and overall vitality. Contains essential vitamins, minerals, and amino acids specific to avian nutritional needs.

  • BiteGuard

    Premium flea and tick protection that's safe for all pets. Our formula provides long-lasting protection against external parasites while being gentle on your pet's skin and system.

Home Treatment Visits

  • Wellness Checkups

    Regular health assessments conducted in the comfort of your home. These comprehensive exams include vital signs monitoring, weight checks, and overall health evaluation.

  • Vaccinations

    Stay current on all necessary vaccinations without the stress of clinic visits. I bring all required vaccines and can administer them safely in your home environment.

  • Urgent Care

    When your pet needs immediate attention but isn't critically ill, I can provide home-based urgent care services to address many common conditions and injuries.

  • Geriatric Care

    Specialized care for senior pets who may have mobility issues or stress easily. I provide gentle examinations and treatments tailored to aging pets' needs.
